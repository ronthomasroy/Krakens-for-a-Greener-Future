SDG 13: Climate Action

About Climate Action

Climate change is one of the greatest challenges facing our planet. It affects every country and can have devastating effects on communities, economies, and ecosystems.

Key Targets:

  • Strengthen resilience to climate-related hazards
  • Integrate climate change measures into policies and planning
  • Improve education and awareness on climate change

SDG 14: Life Below Water

About Life Below Water

Our oceans cover more than 70% of the Earth's surface and play a crucial role in maintaining the planet's ecosystems. However, they face numerous threats from human activities.

Key Targets:

  • Reduce marine pollution
  • Protect and restore marine ecosystems
  • Regulate harvesting and end overfishing

    • Explore Resources for Life Below Water

    Learn how Earth observation data is helping protect marine ecosystems and improve sustainability:

    • Earth Observations for Sustainable Development Goals (EO4SDG)
    • UNEP Ozone Secretariat - Protecting the Ozone Layer for a Sustainable Future

SDG 15: Life on Land

About Life on Land

Terrestrial ecosystems are vital for all life on Earth. Forests, wetlands, mountains, and drylands form intricate webs of life that provide food, water, air, and shelter for countless species, including humans.

Key Targets:

  • Protect and restore terrestrial ecosystems
  • Combat desertification and restore degraded land
  • Halt biodiversity loss and protect endangered species

    • Learn More About Life on Land

    Discover tools and research that are helping protect terrestrial ecosystems and biodiversity:
